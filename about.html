About Me

I'm a twenty-three year old informatics student at Arizona State University. I am currently a senior at ASU about to start my final semester here at the university. I am an inspiring video game developer, artist, and 3D modeler. My ultimate dream would be to help develop and create 3D assets for blockbuster games. I focus mostly on science fiction weaponry and personell, but have a taking for creating creatures and monsters as well.


I have a loving family my mother Valentina, my Dad Juan, my older brother Juan, and my loving girlfriend Sarah. Throw in seven dogs, and well let the madness begin.


Outside of school and work, I am an avid gamer and Star Wars nerd. I have a passion for video games, and I am an active gamer in the community playing a wide variety of games from shooters, to RPGs.


I am a member of the local Star Wars charity groups, the Mandalorian Mercs, 501st, and the Rebel Legion. We travel around Arizona and the United States to dress up as various Star Wars characters to help raise money and awarness for countless other charities and their causes. I have been involved with the organization since August of 2010 and have never looked back sense. The experiences I have had being a member of these groups have changed my prospective in life.
